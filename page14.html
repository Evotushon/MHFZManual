COG Member Registration Procedure

You can register as a member of COG at the following page. Please refer to this page to register.

Free member registration (link to COG)

(1) Entering membership information

Fill in the [Register Customer Information] fields, including your e-mail address, password, and date of birth for registration with COG. Also, specify how you would like to receive the latest information from Capcom Online Games. Finally, carefully read the [COG Membership Agreement]. If you agree to the terms and conditions, check the box below the COG Membership Agreement and click the [Register with these details] button.

 

COG ID and COG password will be issued automatically. Please refer to the COG official website for information on how to change to the optional content.

 

COG：よくあるご質問

 

The "Register your information" field is used to verify your identity when you contact us, for example, if you have lost your COG ID or password.

 

(2) Registration completed

The registration completion screen will appear and your membership registration will be completed. At the same time, an e-mail notifying you of the completion of your membership registration will be sent to your registered e-mail address.
