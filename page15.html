COG Trial Course Registration Procedure

How to register for the trial course (free of charge)

The "Trial Course" is a free course with limited functions. In this course, your Hunter Rank (HR) will only increase to 5, and you will only be able to receive some quests.
In order to play the various contents of the game, you will need to purchase the Hunter Life Course and Continous Hunter Life Course.

Here we first explain how to register for a trial course.

How to register for the trial course (free of charge)

 

(1) Log in with your registered COG ID.

(2) After logging in, the screen will change as follows.

(3)　Click on "Buy Subscription."

(4)　Click on "Proceed to Purchase Page" for "Monster Hunter Frontier Z".

(5)　Click the "Register" button under "Trial Course (Free).

(6)　Read the "Terms of Use" carefully, check the "I agree to the Terms of Use" checkbox, and then click "Purchase.

※If you do not agree to the terms and conditions of use, you may not use the site.

(7)　Registration finished!

　If you get to this screen, your registration is complete. Thank you for your time!
